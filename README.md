# Business Analysis Report: Sales Performance & Profitability Assessment

## Executive Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive business analysis examining sales performance, profitability metrics, and strategic opportunities across a four-year analysis period (2023-2026). The analysis synthesizes data from 5,111 transactions representing $2,326,534 in revenue across 804 customers.

**Key Finding:** The organization demonstrates strong year-over-year growth of 14.9% with healthy customer retention metrics. However, profitability is significantly impacted by two critical operational issues: excessive discounting practices and structural challenges in the furniture category. Strategic intervention in these areas presents an opportunity to increase annual profit by $275,000-$400,000.

### 2. **Critical Issues Identified**

#### Issue A: Discount Structure Impact
- **Scope:** 20% of orders (1,039 transactions) feature discounts exceeding 20%
- **Financial Impact:** Net loss of $136,021 annually on heavily discounted orders
- **Root Cause:** Absence of discount authorization controls and ceiling policies
- **Recommendation:** Implement maximum discount cap of 10% across all categories

#### Issue B: Furniture Category Profitability
- **Scope:** Furniture represents 32.4% of revenue but generates only 2.61% profit margin
- **Financial Impact:** $754,748 in revenue yields only $19,730 in profit
- **Root Cause:** Combination of elevated average discount rate (17.30%) and structural cost challenges
- **Recommendation:** Conduct competitive price analysis and implement strategic pricing adjustment or product line rationalization

#### Issue C: Geographic Concentration Risk
- **Scope:** Top three markets (California, New York, Texas) account for 40.4% of revenue
- **Risk Level:** Exceeds acceptable concentration threshold (recommended maximum: 35%)
- **Mitigation Strategy:** Geographic diversification initiative targeting underserved Southern markets

## Strategic Recommendations

**A. Discount Policy Standardization**

- Require director-level approval for exceptions
- Eliminate all discounts exceeding 15% without executive review
- **Expected Annual Impact:** $200,000-$300,000 profit recovery

**B. Furniture Category Strategic Review**
- Conduct competitive pricing benchmark analysis
- Evaluate supply chain cost optimization opportunities
- Implement decision framework within 30 days:
  - Option 1: Price adjustment (20-30% increase)
  - Option 2: Discount restructuring (reduce from 17.3% to 10%)
  - Option 3: Product line rationalization (discontinue unprofitable items)
- **Expected Annual Impact:** $75,000-$100,000 profit improvement

**C. February Revenue Stabilization Campaign**

- Implement bundled product offerings
- Deploy targeted customer incentive programs
